How to Buy

1

Download Metamask

Download the MetaMask mobile app or install the extension on desktop. Setup your MetaMask wallet to be able to store your tokens.
2

Deposit $ETH

Deposit $ETH (ERC20) into your MetaMask wallet using any crypto exchange of your choice or purchase within the app.
3

Buy Using Metamask

Go to Uniswap, connect your wallet and paste the $Cloud token address into Uniswap. Now swap your ETH into $Cloud.
